The Process

What Happens After You Submit

The moment you hit send, the agent team gets to work. Here's exactly what happens — and which agent handles each step.

1

Instant Confirmation — within 60 seconds

HERALD

HERALD agent sends you a personalized confirmation email and SMS within 60 seconds of submission — including a brief intake questionnaire and what to expect next.

2

Preliminary Digital Audit begins immediately

PULSE

PULSE agent immediately begins scanning your digital presence — website, Meta profile, SEO standing, and any existing ad accounts — building a baseline performance picture.

3

Competitive landscape research

SCOUT

SCOUT agent maps your ministry's competitive landscape — similar organizations in your area, what they're advertising, what content is performing in your niche, and where the gaps are.

4

Andrew + DIRECTOR review all findings

DIRECTOR

DIRECTOR agent compiles the PULSE audit and SCOUT research into a unified brief. Andrew reviews every finding personally, adds strategic context, and schedules your strategy call.

5

Pre-call briefing delivered 24 hours before your call

HERALD

You receive a pre-call briefing document 24 hours before your strategy session — summarizing what the agents found, what Andrew will cover, and what questions to have ready. You arrive prepared, not surprised.
