The Challenge

Faith-based organizations receive a constant stream of inquiries — event RSVPs, prayer requests, counseling inquiries, partnership emails, media inquiries. Responding promptly and personally is critical to mission integrity, but the volume overwhelms small teams.

Most organizations either respond 2–3 days late (damaging trust) or default to generic copy-paste replies (damaging relationships). Neither reflects Sonship.

The Solution

HERALD agent, powered by N8N + Claude API, monitors the client's Gmail inbox every 60 seconds. When a new email arrives, the AI reads the full thread, analyzes sentiment (positive, negative, unclear), and drafts a personalized, theology-consistent reply.

The drafted reply is sent to Andrew — or the client's designated approver — via Telegram bot: "New email from [Sender]. Here is the drafted reply. Reply 'ok' to send." Nothing goes out without human eyes on it. AI speed, human wisdom.

The Challenge

A church plant needed a website that felt premium, modern, and deeply reflective of their identity — not a $49/month template that looks like every other church in town. Their previous agency quoted 6 weeks and $8,000.

The Solution

Andrew ran a Vibe Design Sprint using YAHWAYLOVE's proprietary AI-native workflow. Instead of asking "What should it look like?", he asked "What do you want visitors to feel when they land on your site?" The answer drove the entire design direction.

Google Stitch generated 5 fully realized, designer-level UI directions in 90 seconds. The client chose in one session. BUILD agent took that direction into Claude Code, scaffolded the full site architecture, and pushed to Vercel. Live in 48 hours — fully custom, zero templates.

The Challenge

Thy Kingdom Come Productions had zero digital ad presence, no lead tracking system, and no funnel to convert event interest into actual registrations. Their audience was engaged on-platform but unreachable through paid channels.

Every event relied on organic word-of-mouth and in-church announcements — a model that plateaued. They were marketing from scarcity, not from the abundance that God had already prepared for their mission.

The Solution

ARIA agent built and launched a complete Meta Ads strategy from scratch: audience research using faith-interest stacking, creative briefs produced by CONTENT, and a structured A/B testing matrix across 3 ad sets simultaneously.

FUNNEL built a custom landing page (BUILD) with HubSpot CRM integration. CONTENT produced a 30-day social calendar to complement the ads. HERALD set up a 5-email nurture sequence for every lead captured. PULSE generated weekly plain-English reports tracking reach, leads, cost-per-lead, and conversion rate.
